Donation Guidelines

  • Must be between 18-65 years of age
  • Minimum weight requirement: 50kg (110 lbs)
  • Wait 90 days between blood donations
  • Bring valid government-issued photo ID
  • Have a light meal 2-3 hours before donation
  • Stay hydrated - drink plenty of water
